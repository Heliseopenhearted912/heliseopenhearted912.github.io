Download OBS Studio 2026

OBS Studio functions as software for video recording and live streaming. Users rely on this tool to capture their screen, manage audio sources, and broadcast content to various platforms. The 2026 version offers stability and performance improvements for Windows 10 and Windows 11 users.

  • At least 4GB of RAM.
  • A modern processor (Intel Core i5 or AMD equivalent).
  • A graphics card that supports DirectX 10.1 or higher.
  • An active internet connection for streaming tasks.

⚙️ Initial Setup

When you open the application for the first time, the Auto-Configuration Wizard appears. This tool helps you choose settings based on your hardware. Select the option that matches your goal, such as Optimizing for Streaming or Optimizing for Recording only. The wizard detects your internet speed and your computer hardware to select the best resolution and frame rate.

🖥️ Adding Sources

The main window shows a preview area. To record your screen, follow these instructions:

  • Step 1: Find the box labeled Sources at the bottom of the screen.
  • Step 2: Click the plus icon in that box.
  • Step 3: Select Display Capture to record your entire screen.
  • Step 4: Select Window Capture if you only want to record one specific program.
  • Step 5: Click OK to confirm your choice.

🎙️ Audio Management

The software detects your microphone and desktop audio automatically. You see these levels in the Audio Mixer panel. Watch the bars while you play sound or speak. If the bars hit the red zone, the audio sounds distorted. You can drag the slider down to lower the volume to a safe level.

🚀 Recording and Streaming

Look at the Controls panel on the right side of the window. Click Start Recording to save your video file to your hard drive. Click Stop Recording when you finish. If you want to stream to a platform, use the Settings menu to link your account. Then click Start Streaming to begin your live broadcast.

📖 Common Tips

Organize your work by using Scenes. You can create different sets of sources for different tasks, like gaming, meetings, or tutorials. Switching between scenes happens with a single click. Save your projects often to prevent data loss. If you encounter issues with software performance, check the logs located in the Help menu.
